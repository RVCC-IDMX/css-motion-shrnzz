Micro Interaction

The button gives a gentle lift and tiny scale (300ms) to communicate interactivity with restraint. Reduced-motion users receive an immediate color and focus change so state remains clear without animation.

Timing Comparison

Linear

Ease-out

Linear moves at a steady, constant rate — it feels mechanical and unforgiving. Ease-out starts a little faster and slows into place, which reads as more natural and responsive; the easing gives the motion a friendly, intentional finish that feels easier to perceive.
